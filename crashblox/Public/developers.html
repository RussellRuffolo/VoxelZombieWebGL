Developers

Russell Ruffolo

One of two Ruffolo brothers on this project, Russell was born and raised in Pittsburgh, PA. He attended Tufts University and graduated in 2020 with a major in Physics and a focus in Computer Science. He is the driving force behind crashblox. His work on the game includes the voxel engine, the movement system, and the client prediction netcode. He also worked extensively on the WebRTC connection and learned enough HTML to write this site.

Drew Ruffolo

The other of two Ruffolo brothers on this project, Drew was also born and raised in Pittsburgh, PA. He is a talented microservices developer whose current work is maintaining a database of public records for the Turtle Creek Council of Governments. His work on crashblox includes the identity server and player database, as well as invaluable contributions to the WebRTC connection. His patience with debugging has also been invaluable.
